Mushroom Goat Cheese Omelette

by Jeff Knighton

4 9

Servings:1

Valentine's day breakfast for my wife

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbs Olive oil
  • 4 Sliced Mushrooms
  • 1/2 Chopped Shallot
  • 1 Tbs Butter
  • 1 Scrambled Egg
  • Pinch Kosher Salt
  • Crumbled Goat Cheese
  • Chopped Green Onion

Cooking Instructions

  • Add 1 Tbs Olive oil to pan
  • Add 4 Sliced Mushrooms to pan
  • Add 1/2 Chopped Shallot to pan
  • Stir 4 Sliced Mushrooms and 1/2 Chopped Shallot
  • Stir 4 Sliced Mushrooms and 1/2 Chopped Shallot
  • Remove 4 Sliced Mushrooms and 1/2 Chopped Shallot from pan
  • Add 1 Tbs Butter to pan
  • Stir 1 Tbs Butter
  • Add 1 Scrambled Egg to pan
  • Stir 1 Scrambled Egg
  • Add Pinch Kosher Salt to pan
  • Add 4 Sliced Mushrooms and 1/2 Chopped Shallot to pan
  • Add Crumbled Goat Cheese to pan
  • Add Chopped Green Onion to pan
  • Flip 1 Scrambled Egg
  • Remove 1 Scrambled Egg from pan
